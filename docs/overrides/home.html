.fmbot

Takes your music listening history to the next level and transforms it into a social experience

Add to server Add to account Docs Join the .fmbot server

A social Discord bot that provides music statistics for you, your friends, and your fellow server members. Discover who listens to your favorite music, get new insights, and explore new music together.

Main features

  • Share what you're currently listening to and personalize your command

    .fm/fm .fmmode/fmmode

  • Generate advanced image charts based on your listening history

    .chart/chart .artistchart/chart artists

  • See your most played artists, albums and tracks for any time period

    .topartists/top artists .topalbums/top albums .toptracks/top tracks

  • Get a complete overview of your listening stats at a glance

    .overview/overview

  • Easily see genre and country info for your favorite artists

    .from/country .countrychart/countrychart .genre/genre

  • Every hour a random user gets featured - the bot changes its profile picture to the album cover

    .featured/featured .featuredlog/featuredlog

  • Play music guessing games together with friends

    .pixel.pixel .jumble.jumble

  • See your user profile with interesting statistics and a yearly overview

    .profile/profile

  • Generate an iceberg based on artist popularity and a track receipt

    .iceberg/iceberg .receipt/receipt

Social features

  • See who in your server listens to an artist, album or track and compete for the top spot

    .whoknows/whoknows .wkalbum/wkalbum .wktrack/wktrack

  • Earn crowns by being the #1 listener of an artist in your server and build your collection

    .crowns/crowns .crownleaderboard/crown

  • Add friends and keep track of what they've been listening to

    .friends/friends .addfriends/addfriend .removefriends/removefriend

  • See how you stack up against anyone in the bot with global leaderboards

    .globalwhoknows/globalwhoknows .gwalbum/gwkalbum .gwtrack/gwktrack

  • See what music is trending in your server for various time periods

    .serverartists/server artists .serveralbums/server albums .servertracks/server tracks

  • Compare your music taste with others and find who listens most like you

    .taste/taste .affinity/affinity

Integrations

  • Get direct links to your music on streaming platforms

    .spotify/spotify .applemusic/applemusic .youtube/youtube

  • Browse your Discogs vinyl collection and show it on your profile

    .discogs/discogs .collection/collection

  • Automatically scrobble music from Discord music bots to your Last.fm

    .botscrobbling/botscrobbling

  • Manually scrobble tracks and manage your loved tracks on Last.fm

    .scrobble/scrobble .love/love .unlove/unlove

  • Look up lyrics for any track you're listening to

    .lyrics/lyrics .genius.genius

  • Import your full Spotify or Apple Music listening history

    .import/import

Get started

  • 1

    Add to your server

    Invite .fmbot to your Discord server, add it to your account or try it out in the .fmbot server

  • 2

    Connect your Last.fm

    Sign up for Last.fm if you haven't already, then use .login/login in Discord to link your account

  • 3

    Start using commands

    Try .fm/fm to share what you're listening to, or browse all commands to explore everything .fmbot can do
