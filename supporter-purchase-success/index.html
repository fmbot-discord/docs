    Overview of your new perks

    📈 Expanded commands with more statistics

    • .profile — Expanded profile with more insights and a yearly overview
    • .recap — Extra pages with discoveries and listening time
    • .recent — See your lifetime listening history and filter to specific artists
    • .artisttracks — See all tracks, even those outside of your top 6000
    • .artistalbums — See all albums, even those outside of your top 5000

    🎮 Enhanced commands

    • .lyrics — View lyrics directly inside of .fmbot
    • .featured — Chance to get featured on Supporter Sunday (First sunday of each month)
    • .judge — Better output and ability to use the command on others
    • .jumble — Play unlimited Jumble games
    • .pixel — Play unlimited Pixel Jumble games

    🔎 Go back in time

    • .discoveries — View your recently discovered artists
    • .gaps — View music you returned to after a gap in listening
    • .discoverydate/.dd — View when you discovered an artist, album and track
    • .artist, .album, .track — See discovery dates

    🗄️ Import your Spotify and/or Apple Music

    • /import spotify — Import your full Spotify History
    • /import applemusic — Import your full Apple Music History
    • /import manage — Configure your imports and how they're used
    • /import modify — Edit and delete artists, albums and tracks in your imports

    ⚙️ More customization

    • /fmmode — Expand your fm footer with more and exclusive options
    • .userreactions — Set your own emote reactions used globally
    • .addfriends — Add up to 18 friends, up from 12

    🌎 Community perks

    • Support hosting and development
    • ⭐ — Supporter badge in the bot
    • Exclusive role and channel on our server

    Please note that .fmbot is not affiliated with Last.fm. Supporter does not grant Last.fm Pro, or the other way around.
